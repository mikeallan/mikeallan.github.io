Well hello there! Welcome to my page to accompany my application for the Associate Product Owner position at Scotiabank. I'm incredibly excited to be applying for this position. I created this webpage along with the rest of my website from scratch, with no preset templates or CSS, except for the landing page - that was bootstrapped... Anyways, let me tell you a bit about myself, what makes me excited about this position, and why I am a great fit!

"Why are you interested in this position?"

It’s an exciting time to be in the financial sector. We are already seeing significant changes with how people interact with banks. With threats from powerful companies such as Amazon and Facebook, banks need to be innovating and rethinking how things have always be done to ensure success against these potential new entrants. That’s why Scotiabank’s Associate Product Owner position is so intriguing to me. I want to be a leader in this revolutionary change. I spoke with Eric Bujold (a current APO) about this position and what the team is trying to do, and it made me even more excited about this position.

"Wait, who are you again?"

I am originally from Newfoundland, and even though I have been to 15 countries, it remains my favourite place in the world. I received the Loran Scholarship in 2013, and moved to Ontario to start school at Western University. I chose to pursue my interest in business, problem solving, and science, enrolling in the dual degree program of Engineering and Business. Ever since, I have been actively involved at school.
