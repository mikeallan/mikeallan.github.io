About Me

Who is Mikie Allan?

I am in my fifth year at Western University, and I'm originally from the fantastic province of Newfoundland. I'm almost finished the dual degree program in Mechatronic Systems Engineering and Honors Business Administration from the Ivey Business School as a Loran Scholar.

I've spent my summers doing a wide array of things. In summer of 2014, I worked at the National Office of Engineers without Borders Canada in Toronto, Canada on their Food Systems portfolio. To see some of what I worked on, check this out. For the summer of 2015, I worked at FairServices Peru as a Sports Coordinator. When not studying Spanish or salsa dancing, I spent a lot of my time travelling around the region and getting to know Cusco. In 2016 I was awarded the Cansbridge Fellowship, which brought me to Shenzhen, China. I worked at Husky Oil China Ltd. as an economics analyst where I created a database that stored Husky's economic and financial data for the Asia pacific region, and created an automated management report generating system through VBA. I spent time outside of work (making an effort at) learning Mandarin, playing ultimate Frisbee, and travelling. For summer 2017, I was back in Toronto working for a Venture-backed financial technology company called FundThrough as the partnerships manager. I have stayed on part time working on automation of different systems.

I'm interested in a wide range of things, including athletics, music, languages, entrepreneurship, technology, and social change. I'm striving to find a way to amalgamate my passions for business, technology, and social justice. To get a hold of me, please head over to the contact page!

What am I currently doing?

Right now, I'm back at school completing my fifth and final year. I'm looking forward to finishing up and getting out to the real world. My capstone project is really exciting - essentailly a contact lens that can detect glaucoma. I chose to take a bit of a step back from extra-curriculars this year, so I can spend more time on school, with friends, and take time to work on some side project. Outside of that, I'm still playing ultimate and volleyball. As well, I'm still Rubik's cubing, salsa dancing, and now rock climbing.
